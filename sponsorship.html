Why advertise here

The arithmetic of a narrow audience

Broad platforms sell you reach and let you sort out relevance. A focused publication sells you relevance and lets reach take care of itself. For a deep-tech firm, the second model is almost always the better trade.

01

Acquisition cost, inverted

Performance channels charge a premium to reach a deep-tech buyer because the audience is rare and the bidding is fierce. Editorial placement reaches the same reader through context, not auction — relevance over CPC bidding wars.

02

Trust transfers

A reader who trusts the publication extends some of that trust to who it carries. A sponsored brief alongside credible analysis lands differently than a banner chasing someone across the web.

03

Attention without the tax

On social and search you rent attention and pay the platform a margin on every impression. Here you reach the audience directly — no intermediary skim.

04

Evergreen, indexed, compounding

A sponsored feature keeps working long after the campaign window — indexed, linkable, surfacing for the exact searches your buyers run. Spend once, surface for years.

Questions

Before you reach out

Is sponsored content labeled?

Always. Sponsored features carry a clear label and meet the same editorial standards as our independent coverage. Reader trust is the asset that makes a placement here worth anything — we protect it.

Do sponsors influence editorial coverage?

No. Advertising and editorial are separated. Sponsorship buys placement and clearly-labeled collaboration — never favorable coverage or influence over what we report independently.

Why aren't prices listed?

Because the right structure varies by category, goal and campaign. A blanket rate card would either overcharge a small placement or undersell a large one. We scope each partnership to fit, then quote.

Who is the audience, really?

A concentrated, B2B deep-tech readership in India — founders, researchers, infrastructure buyers and investors. Narrow by design. If that's who you sell to, it's a strong fit; if you need mass consumer reach, it isn't.
